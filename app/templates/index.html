The Challenge

Addressing Critical Gaps in Health Supply Chains

Many countries face significant challenges in managing their health supply chains, leading to critical issues that affect public health.

  • Shortages & Delays: Essential commodities like vaccines and medicines are often unavailable when needed most.
  • Inaccurate Forecasting: Traditional, manual forecasting methods are prone to error and fail to leverage valuable data insights.
  • Public Health Risks: Delays in the supply chain can have severe consequences, impacting patient care and emergency response.

Our Solution

We aim to develop an Ensemble Framework that integrates LSTM architectures with attention-based models to enhance country-specific demand forecasting. Our system uses a Lookback Window method to effectively capture temporal patterns and predict future demand with improved accuracy.

ARIMA / SARIMA

Utilized for its strength in univariate time series forecasting, capturing trends and seasonality in historical data.

XGBoost

A powerful gradient boosting algorithm that handles non-linear relationships in multivariate forecasting.

LSTM Network

A deep learning model (Long Short-Term Memory) specifically designed for sequence prediction and capturing long-term dependencies.

Random Forest

An ensemble model used for its robustness, high accuracy, and ability to provide interpretable predictions.

Technical Approach & Analysis

Our methodology is rooted in advanced time-series analysis and ensemble learning to provide the most accurate forecasts possible.

Core Methodology

  • Ensemble Framework: We don't rely on a single model. Our system combines the strengths of multiple algorithms to produce a more robust and accurate final prediction.
  • LSTM with Attention: At the core is a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) network, perfect for time-series data, enhanced with an attention mechanism to focus on important historical data.
  • Lookback Window: The model analyzes a "window" of past data to effectively capture trends, seasonality, and complex temporal patterns.
